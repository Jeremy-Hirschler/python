Rules for BlackJack:

  1. Get a total score of 21 or as close to 21 without going over.
  2. After each new card dealt to the player, player can choose to 'Hit' or 'Stand'.
  3. Dealer must 'Hit' if their total score is under 17 and 'Stand' if above 17.
  4. Player must beat the dealer (house) to win.
